❗ The Real Problem

If your child studies hard but still scores 50–80% in Maths and Science

The problem is NOT effort alone. It's the lack of the right system.

❌

Conceptual Clarity

Memorising without understanding. Students rote-learn formulas but can't apply them when questions are framed differently.

❌

Application Skills

Wrong application of concepts. Knowing a concept is very different from knowing when and how to use it in exam conditions.

❌

Revision System

Passive revision — reading notes once — builds no lasting retention. What's learned in April is forgotten by exam time.

❌

Presentation Skills

Poor representation in board papers costs step marks. Knowing the answer isn't enough — how it's written decides the score.

❌

Exam Strategy

Unaware of the right study resources. Students waste time on low-yield content while missing what the CBSE paper actually tests.

❌

Marking Scheme

Unaware of words-per-mark ratios. Students write too little or too much — and lose marks to examiners even when they know the subject.
